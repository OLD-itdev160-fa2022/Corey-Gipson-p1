Who Is J?

Welcome to All Styles By J, your number one source for all styles of hair. Jasmin dedicated to giving you the very best of hair styles, while making the your visit very enjoyable.

Founded in 2019 by Jasmin, All Styles By J has come a long way from its beginnings in 2201 N. Martin Luther King Dr. When Jas first started out, her passion for doing hair at an affordable price drove her to start taking her business seriously and go full time. All Styles By J offers you professional hair styles at a modest price . She now serve customers all over Milwaukee, and she is thrilled that she is able to turn our passion her own website.

I hope you enjoy the experiences while getting your hair done as much as I enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact me.
